# Delete your Videx account

This page explains how to delete your **Videx: Streaming Guide** account
(published by Joe Green, `app.videx.streaming`) and what happens to your
data when you do. Deletion is available to everyone, free, and does not
require you to contact us first.

## Delete in the app (instant)

1. Open Videx and go to the **Profile** tab.
2. Tap **Privacy & Data**.
3. Tap **Delete my account**.
4. Type your username to confirm, then tap **Delete**.

Deletion is immediate and permanent. You'll be signed out and the
account cannot be recovered.

## Delete without the app (email)

If you no longer have the app installed, email
**privacy@videxstreaming.com** from the email address on the account and
ask for deletion. We verify the request by replying to the account's
address, then delete the account. We aim to complete email requests
within 7 days, and in any case within one month as UK GDPR requires.

## What is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes, at the moment of deletion:

- your account and sign-in identity (email address and password hash)
- your profile (username, optional age range and viewing context)
- your selected streaming services and genre preferences
- your watchlist and watched history
- your taste profile (the recommendation model built for you)
- your app-activity records (interaction events, card impressions,
  onboarding analytics)
- your push-notification tokens, notification preferences and delivery
  records
- your availability reports and feedback submissions
- your feature flags

## What is kept

- **Encrypted backups** of our database are retained for up to 90 days
  for disaster recovery. Your deleted data ages out of these
  automatically within that window; backups are never used to
  reconstruct deleted accounts.

Nothing else is retained. We do not sell or share personal data, so
there are no third parties to propagate deletion to beyond our
processing infrastructure (Supabase), where deletion takes effect
immediately.

## Questions

Email **privacy@videxstreaming.com**. Our full privacy policy is at
[videxstreaming.com/privacy](https://videxstreaming.com/privacy).
