## 2. What Videx is

Videx is a personal recommendation aggregator for UK streaming
services. It surfaces films and TV shows from your existing
subscriptions based on your taste, and links out to the streaming
services you already pay for so you can watch them there.

Videx does NOT stream any content itself. We are not affiliated

- Availability data is real-time accurate (TMDb and the Streaming
